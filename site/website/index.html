IlanSQL Exercises

Welcome to IlanSQL — Where the Brightest Minds Become Masters of SQL in Antarctica’s Digital Command Center!

IlanSQL was forged to transform the most promising young penguin-cadets into SQL warriors — the ones who will defend the country’s data against growing cyberthreats. While many resources exist to learn SQL, few enable you to become a true expert by putting your skills to the test. IlanSQL does exactly that. Inside IlanSQL, you’ll work with a realistic, enterprise-grade dataset and solve a series of technical challenges — starting from fundamentals with SELECT and WHERE clauses, progressing through GROUP BY, HAVING, WINDOW FUNCTIONS, JOINS, CASTING, INDEXING, VIEWS, CTEs (common table expressions), up to complex recursive queries and performance optimization strategies.

Whether you’re a beginner eager to conquer your first INNER JOIN or a veteran looking to fine-tune your window function prowess, IlanSQL prepares you for battle in a world where data is power — and the strongest SQL practitioners hold the key

To deploy your training, select a scenario from the menu and let the icy waters of Antarctica sharpen your technical expertise.
